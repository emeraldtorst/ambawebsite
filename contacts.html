Frequently Asked Questions

How can I become a member of AMBA?

You can apply for membership through our website or by contacting our office directly. We offer different membership levels for individuals, businesses, and organizations.

Do I need to be Austrian or Mongolian to join?

No, AMBA welcomes anyone with an interest in Austrian-Mongolian relations, regardless of nationality. Our members include people from various backgrounds who share our mission.

How often do you hold events?

We typically hold several events each month, including networking sessions, cultural events, business forums, and educational workshops. Check our events calendar for upcoming activities.

Can I volunteer with AMBA?

Yes, we welcome volunteers to help with events, outreach, and various projects. Please contact us to learn about current volunteer opportunities.
