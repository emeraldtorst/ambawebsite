Our Mission

The Austrian Mongolian Business Association (AMBA) serves as a platform for fostering economic ties, facilitating business opportunities, and promoting cultural understanding between Austria and Mongolia.

Business Networking

Creating opportunities for Austrian and Mongolian businesses to connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Cultural Exchange

Promoting mutual understanding through cultural events, language programs, and educational initiatives.

Economic Development

Supporting investment opportunities and economic growth between our two nations.

Join Our Community

Become part of a growing network of professionals, entrepreneurs, and cultural enthusiasts dedicated to strengthening Austrian-Mongolian relations.
